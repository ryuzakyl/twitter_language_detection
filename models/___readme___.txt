Each folder stores the models for each language or category. The categories used are:
. [de] - German
. [en] - English
. [es] - Spanish
. [fr] - French
. [it] - Italian

The names of the models stored for each language (category) have the folowing conventions:
[source]-[feature_type].txt

where:
. 'source' is the owner or source that generated the model
. 'feature_type' is the type(s) of feature(s) considered in the model

Note: The '3rdparty' models do not add any padding at the beggining nor the end of the text.
